The Platform

From a new element set to an assessed event.

Everyone can see that a satellite moved; the tracking data is public. Vantage is the layer that tells you why, and hands you the evidence to stand behind it.

  • 01

    Ingest

    Open connectors normalize public catalogs and element-set histories into one data fabric. No proprietary sensor required.

  • 02

    Detect

    Physics subtracts what nature does (Earth's bulge, atmospheric drag). What's left is somebody's engine: detected, measured, timestamped.

  • 03

    Assess

    Models name the intent, score the threat, and check the behavior against the object's own pattern of life, with confidence and a written rationale.

  • 04

    Report

    The event lands in the feed with its evidence attached: reasoning an analyst, or an auditor, can check, from models graded against a sealed assurance gate.
