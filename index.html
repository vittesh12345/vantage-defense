Capabilities

One autonomy stack. Every stage of the kill chain.

Vantage collapses sensing, fusion, and decision-making into a single edge-to-cloud stack that runs where the mission is — disconnected, denied, or contested.

01

Multi-Sensor Fusion

Ingest RF, EO/IR, radar, SIGINT, and orbital tracks into a single, deconflicted picture of truth — updated in real time.

02

Intent Classification

On-device models score behavior and classify intent — station-keeping, maneuver, rendezvous, or anomaly — with confidence and rationale.

The Platform

From raw sensor feed to accountable action.

Vantage OS is the software backbone that unifies your sensors, models, and operators into one closed loop — deployable in weeks, not years.

  • 01

    Ingest

    Open, schema-driven connectors normalize any sensor or catalog into a common data fabric.

  • 02

    Fuse

    Correlate tracks across domains and time to build a single, deconflicted operational truth.

  • 03

    Reason

    Autonomous models detect, classify, and forecast — surfacing only what demands a decision.

  • 04

    Act

    Route severity-ranked alerts and courses of action to the operator, with full audit trail.
